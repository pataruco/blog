Everyone, at some point, needs a taste of home. For the millions of Venezuelans living abroad, finding specific ingredients—like Harina P.A.N., queso de mano, or Pirulin—can be a challenge.

El Guacal aims to be the largest open database of Venezuelan commercial footprints. It helps diaspora members locate products that connect them to their culture.

The information comes from the public—anyone can go to the repository and add, edit, or remove locations.
